TXPick Guide

What is TXPick?

TXPick helps busy people remember bills, payroll, reminders, appointments, expenses, and daily tasks in one simple app.

Use TXPick when you are busy and do not want to forget important things.

  • Set phone reminders for daily tasks, medicine, appointments, and family plans.
  • Track bills and due dates so payments do not depend on memory.
  • Log personal and business expenses quickly.
  • Keep small business tasks like payroll, supplies, and weekly reminders organized.

Who is it for?

TXPick is useful for busy families, small business owners, nail salon owners, gig workers, and anyone who wants a simple daily organizer without a complicated setup.
