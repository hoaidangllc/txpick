How TXPick Works

TXPick is built to be simple: add what you need to remember, turn on notifications, and check Today.

1. Add a reminder or bill

Create reminders for tasks, bills, appointments, medicine, payroll, or business items.

2. Enable phone notifications

Install TXPick as a PWA on iPhone or Android, then allow notifications so reminders can appear on your phone.

3. Use Today as your daily home

The Today page helps you see what matters now: reminders, bills, expenses, and useful notes.
